Ethilemma is to encourage people to question, acknowledge, and grow.
By presenting ethical dilemmas and inviting diverse viewpoints, the platform nurtures critical thinking and empathy.
It is not about winning arguments or proving superiority; instead, it is about learning from others, exploring different angles, and deepening one’s own moral and philosophical awareness.
